Sagittarum

AI-Powered Grading for Educators and Students

About Sagittarum

Sagittarum is a local-first AI grading platform designed to help teachers save time and help students learn faster. Teachers create assignments and classrooms; students submit work and receive instant AI-powered feedback with detailed explanations.

What We Do

For Teachers: Create classrooms, design assignments with custom grading criteria, and review student submissions with AI-generated explanations and breakdowns.

For Students: View your classes, complete assignments, get graded instantly, and use the "Discuss Mistakes" feature to understand your errors with AI teacher and student explanations.
